About this schedule
  • 3-page Primavera P6 Gantt covering full mechanical & piping construction for dual Prism Hydrogen Generator (PHG830-1 & -2) units at Sangkulirang, East Kalimantan for Air Products PSS Oleo Indonesia.
  • Schedule hierarchy structured by WBS: Off-Site Fabrication (Workshop Gresik) → Mobilization to Site → Construction on Site → Precommissioning, with over 90 activities and logical dependencies.

  • Four schedule data types displayed: Actual Level of Effort, Actual Work, Remaining Work, Critical Remaining Work — as issued at baseline date 25 Dec 2023.

About this schedule
  • 5-page Microsoft Project master schedule for Engineering & Construction to replace the 54" seawater pipeline at AMMAN Mineral's Concentrator Area, Sumbawa Barat — issued as Rev. 02, Week Period 35 (25–31 Aug 2025).
  • 230-day project spanning Jan–Aug 2025. Schedule covers full scope: Preliminary works, Design Engineering, Civil Works (pedestals, platforms), Steel Structure, Pipe Bend & Straight Segments, NDE, and Demobilization.
  • Five pipe straight segments and five pipe bend zones tracked individually — each with Survey/Measuring, Stringing, Fit-up, Welding & Finishing, and Tie-In sub-activities. Non-Destructive Examination (NDE) tracked per segment.
  • Baseline vs. Actual Finish dates tracked across all 173 tasks — project reported at 100% completion by Week 35, with Actual Finish of 31 Aug 2025 vs Initial Schedule of 31 May 2025.
  • Prepared and maintained by Risang Bagaskara Gumilar as Project Control Coordinator on behalf of PT. Swadaya Graha (Client: AMMAN Mineral Nusa Tenggara).
